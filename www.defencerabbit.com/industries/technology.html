Data leak detection

Protect your customer data

Your organization is responsible for safeguarding customer and user data, making it a target for cyber attackers.
  • Prevent data breaches
    Don't be in the news for the wrong reasons. UpGuard's proprietary Data Leak Search Engine scans the surface, deep and dark web, and identifies data that presents a risk.
  • Vendor data leak detection
    Don't let a vendor leak your data. Provide UpGuard with a list of vendors you want to monitor, and get notified if any of them start leaking data.
Third-party risk

Reduce third-party risk to your organization

UpGuard helps you assess, remediate and manage third-party risks across all of your vendors.
  • Continuous vendor monitoring
    Unlike traditional third-party risk solutions, which are based on manual spreadsheets, UpGuard is constantly monitoring your vendors for security exposures.
  • Risk assessment workflows
    Every vendor risk matters. UpGuard automates your risk assessment workflows, accelerating decision making, and helping you to assess, waive or create actionable remediation plans that are fully auditable.

Continuous monitoring

Manage your attack surface

Conduct continuous cyber and business monitoring to reveal potential vendor risks and inform prioritization and risk awareness.
  • Comprehensive security ratings
    Our security ratings provide a data-driven, objective, and dynamic measurement of your security posture. We use trusted commercial, open-source, and proprietary threat intelligence feeds, and non-intrusive data collection methods.
  • Instant onboarding
    Get real-time information about misconfigurations, understand your risk profile, and get started in minutes, not weeks. Because we use externally verifiable information, you won’t have to lift a finger to get started.
Regulatory compliance

Check the regulatory boxes

Achieve compliance for ISO27001, GDPR, NIST and others by using UpGuard security questionnaires for your vendors.
  • Industry standard security frameworks
    Assess your own organization as well as your third-party vendors. Our questionnaires map to global security frameworks such as ISO27001, GDPR and NIST SP 800-53.
  • Risk assessment workflows
    Every vendor risk matters. UpGuard automates your risk assessment workflows, accelerating decision making, and helping you to assess, waive or create actionable remediation plans that are fully auditable.
